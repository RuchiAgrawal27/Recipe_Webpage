-:Sweet Corn Pulao:-

About Me :

A perfect dish for lunch and dinner. Sweet corn pulao is nutritious and easy to make indian dish. The star ingredients which differentiates this Indian corn pulao recipe from
other similar pulao are sweet corn kernels, basmati rice and Indian spices. Make this tasty dish by following step by step process.

Preparation Time : 15minutes
Coocking Time : 20minutes
Serves: 2 servings

Ingredients :

1/2 cup Long Grained rice(Basmati Rice)
3/4 cup sweet corn kernels
1 small piece of bay leaf
2 small piece of Cinnamon stick
2 Cloves
1 large sliced Onion
1 teaspoons crushed Ginger-Garlic
1 sliced Green chilli
1/4 teaspoon Garam Masala
1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder
1 1/5 Cup Water
Salt
1/2 tablespoon Butter or Ghee
1/2 tablespoon Oil
2 tablespoons chopped Coriander leaves

Directions :


Step 1. Wash rice and soak them in water for 15 minutes. If possilbe, soak them for 30 minutes.


Step 2. Heat Oil and Butter in a pan over medium flame. Add bay leaf, Cinnamon stick and Cloves and allow cloves to crackls.
Add sliced onion and saute until it turns transparent.Add crushed Ginger-Garlic and Green chilli and saute until garlic turns light brown.


Step 3. Add sweet corn kernels and saute for a minute. Add garam masal Powder, Coriander Powder and mix well.


Step 4. Drain the excess water from soaked rice and add it.


Step 5. Saute them for 1-2 minutes over medium flame.


Step 6. Add salt and 1 1/5 cup water and bring it to a boil over medium flame.


Step 7. When it starts boiling, reduce flame to low and cover the pan with a lid. Cook coverd for 10 minutes. Don't open the lid in between.


Step 8. Turn off the flame and let it stand for 8-10 minutes. Now, Open the lid and fluff the rice with a fork to separate the rice grains.


Step 9.Transfer prepared sweet corn pulao into a serving bowl and garnish with Coriander leaves.
